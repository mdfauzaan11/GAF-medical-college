About GAF Medical College

Founded with a mission to provide the highest standard of medical education, GAF Medical College has been a leader in healthcare education. Our faculty is committed to empowering students to become compassionate, skilled healthcare professionals.

Our Programs

GAF Medical College offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed in today's healthcare environment.

MBBS – Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery

A comprehensive program that combines medical theory and practical clinical experience to prepare students for a career in medicine.

BDS – Bachelor of Dental Surgery

This program provides students with in-depth training in dental sciences, dental procedures, and patient care.

BSc Nursing

Our BSc Nursing program prepares students for a fulfilling career in nursing, emphasizing patient care, clinical skills, and healthcare systems.

B.Pharm – Pharmacy

This program offers extensive training in pharmaceutical sciences, drug development, and healthcare management.

MD / MS – Postgraduate Specializations

We offer a range of postgraduate programs in specialized areas of medicine, designed to refine the skills and knowledge of experienced medical professionals.

Cut-off Marks

'Kam se kam cut-off hai hamare college ka. Baccho ka poora khayal kre.' The cut-off marks for admission to GAF Medical College vary each year based on the number of applicants and their performance in the entrance examinations. Below are the approximate cut-off marks for the current academic year:

  • MBBS: 300 + in NEET (General Category)
  • BDS: 250 + in NEET (General Category)
  • BSc Nursing: 5000 rank in EAPCET (General Category)
  • B.Pharm: 4000 rank in EAPCET (General Category)
  • MD/MS: Varies by specialization

Please note that these ranks are indicative and may change each year. For the most accurate and up-to-date information, please refer to the official admission notifications or contact the admissions office.
